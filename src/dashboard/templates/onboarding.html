Welcome to Echo Flow

A 30-second tour. Local-only. Nothing leaves your machine.

  1. Push-to-talk

    Hold Ctrl+Shift anywhere. Speak. Release to paste cleaned text into the focused window. Change the binding in Settings → General.

  2. Make it yours

    Add jargon and names in Dictionary so Whisper stops mishearing them. Shortcodes live in Snippets.

  3. Watch it learn

    Every correction you make becomes a few-shot example. Insights shows fixes over time and your most-corrected apps.

  4. Long-form? Use Scratchpad

    Open Scratchpad, click Dictate here, then talk — the dictation appends to the doc instead of pasting into a window.
